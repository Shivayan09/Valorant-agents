What are

agents

In Valorant, an Agent is a unique character with special abilities and a distinct role: Duelist, Initiator, Controller, or even Sentinel. Each agent brings a different playstyle and tactical advantage, making them key to team strategy and success in every match

Duelists :

Self-sufficient fraggers who are expected to secure kills and entry space

jett

Agile and evasive, Jett uses speed as well as verticality in order to outmaneuver enemies and take swift fights

reyna

Thrives on kills, using soul orbs to heal or become invulnerable, making her deadly in clutch fights

phoenix

A fiery entry fragger who can heal himself with his own fire-based abilities and flash around corners

raze

Explosive and aggressive, Raze uses grenades and a rocket launcher to clear areas and secure kills

Initiators :

Create opportunities by disrupting enemies and setting up their team

sova

A recon expert with drones and shock arrows that reveal and damage enemies from afar

breach

Uses seismic charges to flash, stun, and displace enemies through walls and obstacles

fade

Haunts and traps enemies with shadow-based abilities, forcing them into vulnerable positions

skye

A support-focused initiator who scouts with a healing touch and guiding flashes through terrain

Controllers :

Experts in shaping the battlefield with smokes and vision control

brim

Reliable and simple, Brimstone calls in targeted smokes and an orbital strike to control space

omen

A shadowy agent who teleports, blinds, and smokes with unpredictable map control

viper

Uses poison and toxic gas to control zones and deny entry with lasting area denial

harbor

Wields water-based abilities to block vision and slow pushes with cascading waves and shields

Sentinels :

Defensive experts that lock down sites and watch flanks

killjoy
